About City Centre Garage

City Centre Garage has already serving the Birmingham and West Midlands region for over 20 years. We are providing reliable, affordable, and transparent car repair and MOT services.

Customers can expect a friendly and professional experience from our team of experienced mechanics, which delivering high-quality service and ensuring your road safety.

Our garage is equipped with the latest technology and tools to handle all types of vehicles, from compact cars to vans and SUVs. We offer a comprehensive range of services, including MOT testing, routine servicing, vehicle repairs, and full health checks.
